About Us

Founded in 2017, BooN Companion Consults is a trusted travel consultancy dedicated to making every journey simple, seamless, and memorable. Built on the belief that travel should be an exciting experience rather than a stressful one, we are committed to providing reliable guidance and professional support to individuals, families, students, and corporate clients.

At Boon Companion Consults, integrity, professionalism, and customer satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do. We understand that every traveler has unique needs, which is why we take a personalized approach, ensuring each client receives the attention and support they deserve.

Guided by our motto, "Your Travel Companion... Have Confidence!", we are more than just a travel consultancy—we are a dependable partner committed to walking with you every step of your journey.

Our Services

We provide fast, reliable, and affordable domestic and international flight bookings with smooth reservation and ticketing support.

Discover exciting destinations with our carefully planned tour packages, tailored to your preferences, budget, and travel style.

Navigating visa requirements can be challenging, so we guide you through documentation, submission, and the full application process.

We help secure comfortable and affordable hotel accommodations that suit your travel plans, location preferences, and budget.

Enjoy a hassle-free arrival and departure with dependable transfers between the airport, hotel, home, or office.

Our consultants provide expert advice on itinerary planning, travel requirements, destination information, and personalized guidance.

We support businesses with efficient travel planning and coordination that keeps trips organized, reliable, and cost-conscious.

Every journey is unique, and we work closely with clients to organize every detail for a seamless and enjoyable travel experience.
